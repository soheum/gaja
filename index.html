GAJA is a thinktank in Korea that performs research related to sustainable city development in the transportation sector. With having its focus on controversial mobility issues in Korea, GAJA works for promoting a sustainable future of public transportation and micromobility.

GAJA Projects

Sustainable Mobility Solution Campaign

Raising public awareness is needed for sustainable mobility solutions and GAJA opens campaigns for a general change of consciousness.

Mobility Startup Sessions

In order to reach a consensus between taxi companies and mobility startups, GAJA constantly promotes sessions for legislation changes

Biannual Mobility Research Report

GAJA publishes mobility research reports twice a year in order to keep track of statistics in the mobility sector in Korea.
